01 About ReviewAi Connect

ReviewAi Connect is a productivity tool built for business owners who manage their reputation on Google.

Keeping up with customer reviews takes time — especially replying thoughtfully, quickly, and in the customer's own language. ReviewAi Connect brings all of a business owner's Google reviews into a single, simple mobile app, suggests professional replies using AI, and lets the owner publish a response to Google Maps with one tap.

Our mission is to help local businesses respond faster, stay professional, and never miss a review.

Connect your Google account. Sign in securely with Google. You grant access to manage your own Business Profile through Google's official consent screen.

2

Fetch your reviews. The app loads the reviews from your verified Google Business Profile and displays them in the app.

04 How We Use the Google Business Profile APIs

ReviewAi Connect uses Google's official Business Profile APIs solely to provide its core feature: helping a business owner manage the reviews on their own verified Google Business Profile.

What we access

With the user's explicit consent (granted through Google's standard OAuth flow using the business.manage scope), the app uses the Business Profile APIs to:

  • Read the business accounts and locations the signed-in user manages.
  • Read the reviews left on the user's own Business Profile, to display them inside the app.
  • Reply to a review by posting the response text that the business owner has reviewed and approved.

Our business reason

We are a software provider building a legitimate tool for business owners to manage their own Business Profile reviews more efficiently. The app does not aggregate, resell, scrape, or expose third-party business data. Each user only ever accesses the Business Profiles they themselves own and manage.

Policy compliance

We use the Business Profile APIs in accordance with Google's Business Profile API policies and Terms of Service. We do not use the APIs for any prohibited purpose, and we never publish a reply without the owner's explicit action.

All review replies are written or approved by the business owner. AI suggestions are optional drafts; the owner always chooses what, if anything, is posted to Google.

05 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information ReviewAi Connect collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Google account details — your email address and name, obtained through the Google sign-in (OAuth) process.
  • Google authorization tokens — secure tokens that let the app access your Google Business Profile on your behalf, so it can read reviews and post the replies you approve.
  • Business information — your business location name and phone number, as listed on your Google Business Profile.
  • Review content — the text and ratings of reviews left on your business, which the app reads to display them and help you reply.
  • Notification token — a device identifier used only to send you push notifications about new reviews.
  • Subscription status — whether you have an active Pro or Agency subscription, managed through Google Play.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your business reviews inside the app.
  • To generate suggested replies and publish a reply you choose to your Google Business Profile.
  • To send you notifications when new reviews are received.
  • To manage your subscription and provide access to paid features.

AI Processing of Reviews

To generate suggested reply options and translations, the text of a review is sent to a third-party AI service (Google's Gemini API) at the moment you request a reply. This text is processed to create the suggestions shown to you and is not used by us to build a public profile of you or your customers.

Third-Party Services

We share information only with the trusted service providers needed to operate the app:

  • Google — for sign-in, the Google Business Profile API, and AI reply generation.
  • RevenueCat & Google Play — to process and verify subscriptions.
  • Expo — to deliver push notifications.
  • Google Cloud & Firebase — to host our service and securely store the data above.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with advertisers.

Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored on secure cloud infrastructure (Google Cloud / Firebase). Access tokens and account details are used only to provide the service. We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Data Retention & Deletion

We keep your information only as long as your account is active. You can:

  • Log out at any time, which removes your notification token and ends the app's session.
  • Revoke access from your Google Account security settings, which immediately stops the app from accessing your Google Business Profile.
  • Request deletion of all data we hold about you by emailing us at the address below. We will delete your data promptly after verifying your request.

Children's Privacy

ReviewAi Connect is intended for business owners and is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy
