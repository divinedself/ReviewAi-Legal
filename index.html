About ReviewAi Connect

ReviewAi Connect is an AI-powered assistant for Telegram. It brings the comments on your channel and the private messages from your customers into one simple mobile app, helps you understand them, and replies in the right tone and language — so you never miss a conversation and your community always feels heard.
