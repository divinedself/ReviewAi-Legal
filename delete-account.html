Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This page explains how to delete your ReviewAi Connect account and the data associated with it. We respect your right to remove your information at any time. You can do this directly inside the app, or by email.

Option A — Delete from inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open SettingsLaunch ReviewAi Connect and tap the gear (Settings) icon in the top-right corner.
  2. Tap "Delete Account & Data"Scroll to the bottom of Settings and tap the Delete Account & Data option.
  3. ConfirmConfirm in the dialog. Your account and all associated data are deleted right away, and you are signed out.

Option B — Request deletion by email

If you no longer have the app installed, send an email from the address linked to your account, with the subject "Delete my account", to the address below. We will verify the request and delete your data within 30 days, confirming by email once complete.

Send your request to
luckyflopbtt@gmail.com

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we remove:

  • your account information (email, name, and account identifier);
  • your settings, including your knowledge base and automation preferences;
  • any stored comments and messages associated with your account;
  • your push-notification token and login sessions;
  • your subscription record held on our side.

What may be retained

We may retain limited information where we are legally required to do so, or as needed to resolve disputes and enforce our agreements. Such data is kept no longer than necessary.

Important about your subscription: deleting your account does not automatically cancel a paid subscription purchased through Google Play. To stop future charges, also cancel the subscription in your Google Play account settings. Billing records held by Google are subject to Google's own policies.

Questions

If you have any questions about deleting your account or data, contact us at luckyflopbtt@gmail.com. For full details on how we handle your information, see our Privacy Policy.
